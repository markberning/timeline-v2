## GLOBAL RULES FOR ALL LATE EGYPT PROMPTS — read before drawing any map in this file

**CRITICAL RULES — read carefully before drawing:**

1. **Draw each label exactly once.** Do not repeat any city name, region name, river name, or annotation anywhere on the map. If you see an opportunity to put the same label in two places, choose only one.
2. **Spell every label exactly as written in the prompt.** Do not invent words. Do not abbreviate. Do not generate any word that is not in the prompt text. (On prior runs Gemini invented words like "cononniat", "reachit", and "Kushi" — do not do this. Every word on the final map must appear verbatim somewhere in this prompt.)
3. **Include the exact site name on every label.** The site name is the word or phrase before the first parenthesis or em-dash. Never drop a site name.
4. **North is at the top of the map. East is to the right.** Place every location according to its real-world geography in this orientation.
5. **All water is light blue.** Seas, bays, and rivers are light blue. Deserts are pale yellow. Land is beige/tan. River floodplains are soft green.
6. **Do not invent any labels, annotations, sites, or features that are not in this prompt.**

**EGYPT ORIENTATION RULES:**

1. North is at the top of the map. South is at the bottom. The Mediterranean Sea is at the top. Upper Egypt and Nubia are toward the bottom.
2. The Nile flows from south (bottom) to north (top). The river runs roughly vertically through the center of the map, fanning out into the Delta before reaching the Mediterranean.
3. **Memphis** is at the southern apex of the Delta, roughly where the Nile begins to branch. Memphis is NORTH of Thebes.
4. **Thebes** (Karnak / Luxor) is in Upper Egypt, roughly 400 miles (640 km) south of Memphis. Thebes is SOUTH of Memphis.
5. **Sais** is in the western Delta, roughly 50 miles (80 km) northwest of Memphis.
6. **Tanis** is in the northeastern Delta.
7. **Pelusium** is the fortress city at the northeastern corner of the Delta, guarding the frontier against invasions from the Sinai.
8. The **First Cataract** at Aswan marks the traditional southern border of Egypt proper.
9. Lower Egypt is the NORTHERN part (the Delta). Upper Egypt is the SOUTHERN part (the Nile valley south of Memphis). "Lower" = downstream/north, "Upper" = upstream/south.
