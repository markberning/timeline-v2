# Zapotec Civilization — Map Prompt Redos (lean spec)

Self-contained, copy-paste-ready Gemini prompts. Lean spec: 4–5 sites max, name-only labels, ≤1 annotation per map. Ch 6, 8 are clean. Ch 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 7 below. The recurring failure on this TL was duplicate region labels (`Mountains (surrounding ring)` drawn twice, `Etla arm` drawn twice, etc.) — the lean spec drops most of these to remove the duplication risk.

---

## Chapter 1 — REDO (lean)

Create a simple, clean map for Chapter 1 of a mobile reading app about Zapotec Civilization. The chapter covers the Y-shaped Valley of Oaxaca, San José Mogote as the dominant pre-Monte Albán village, and the three-valley rivalry.

The chapter title "Chapter 1: The Valley Before the Mountain" should appear at the top of the map image, centered, in a clean serif font, inside a header bar that spans the full width of the image. The header bar is the ONLY bordered element of the image. The left, right, and bottom edges of the image have NO border or frame — the map geography extends fully to all three of those edges, edge-to-edge. Do not draw a rectangular frame around the map on the left, right, or bottom sides.

**CRITICAL RULES:**

1. **Each region label appears EXACTLY ONCE.** No `Mountains (surrounding ring)` duplicated.
2. **Draw each label exactly once.**
3. **Spell every label exactly as written below.**
4. **Site labels are name-only — no parentheticals on the dots.**
5. **North is at the top. East is to the right.**
6. **All water is light blue.**
7. **Do not invent any labels, sites, or features that are not in this prompt.**

**Map requirements:**
- The Valley of Oaxaca, an upside-down Y with three arms (Etla north, Tlacolula east, Zimatlán south) ringed by mountains
- Muted warm palette

**Sites — 2 dots, name-only labels:**
- **San José Mogote** — Etla arm
- **Monte Albán ridge** — central junction (the future capital, still bare in 1500 BCE)

**Region labels (gray, 5 max — each appears ONCE):**
- Etla arm
- Tlacolula arm
- Zimatlán arm
- Valley of Oaxaca
- Mountains (surrounding ring) ← appears ONCE only

**One annotation:**
- Three shaded zones in the three arms with this single caption: "Three-valley rivalry, ~900–500 BCE — competing territories that need a shared center. Olmec-era greenstone and iconography arrive from the Gulf Coast 300+ miles away."

**Style:** Landscape orientation, simple and elegant.
