5. Damascus is in southern SYRIA, NORTH of Arabia, EAST of the Mediterranean coast. Jerusalem is SOUTHWEST of Damascus.
6. Mesopotamia (modern Iraq) is to the NORTHEAST of Arabia, with the Tigris and Euphrates rivers running NORTH-to-SOUTH and merging near the Persian Gulf. The Euphrates is the WESTERN river. The Tigris is the EASTERN river. Kufa and Basra are in southern Iraq near the Euphrates.
7. Egypt is to the WEST of Arabia, with the Nile flowing SOUTH-TO-NORTH into the Mediterranean. Fustat (later Cairo) is at the apex of the Nile delta. Alexandria is on the Mediterranean coast.
8. Iran is EAST of Mesopotamia, across the Zagros Mountains running NORTHWEST-to-SOUTHEAST. Khorasan is in northeastern Iran. Merv is in modern Turkmenistan, far east. Bukhara and Samarkand are FURTHER east in Transoxiana, beyond the Oxus River.
9. North Africa runs WEST from Egypt along the Mediterranean coast: Libya, Tunisia (where Kairouan was founded), Algeria, Morocco. Spain is across the strait of Gibraltar to the NORTHWEST.
