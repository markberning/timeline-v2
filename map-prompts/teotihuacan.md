4. **North is at the top of the map. East is to the right.** Place every location according to its real-world geography in this orientation.
5. **All water is light blue.** Seas, bays, lakes, and rivers are light blue. Land is beige/tan. Highland terrain is light brown.
6. **Do not invent any labels, annotations, sites, or features that are not in this prompt.**

**MESOAMERICA ORIENTATION RULES:**

1. North is at the top. East is to the right.
2. The **Basin of Mexico** (Valley of Mexico) is in CENTRAL Mexico, at ~7,400 feet elevation. Modern Mexico City fills the basin floor.
3. **Teotihuacan** is ~30 miles (48 km) NORTHEAST of modern Mexico City, in a side valley branching off the northern edge of the Basin of Mexico.
4. The **Gulf of Mexico** is to the EAST. The **Pacific Ocean** is to the WEST/SOUTHWEST.
5. **Monte Albán** (Oaxaca) is ~250 miles (400 km) to the SOUTHEAST.
6. The **Maya lowlands** (Guatemala, Yucatán) are FAR TO THE SOUTHEAST, ~630+ miles from Teotihuacan.
